About Us
Professional Service.
Across the Stars.
The Velantis Group is a civilian service organization operating across the Stanton, Nyx, and Pyro systems. We specialize in one thing: making sure no pilot is ever left stranded. Whether you've run dry in the middle of a quantum jump, need fast extraction from a hostile zone, or require logistical support for a major operation — we dispatch.
Unlike combat-focused organizations, our strength lies in speed, coordination, and professionalism. Every request goes through our dispatch center, where available pilots are assigned based on proximity and specialization. Payment is handled directly in-game — via the refueling mechanic or aUEC transfer — with no upfront commitment required.
Founded on the principle that the 'verse is better when pilots look out for each other, Velantis Group is built by players, for players.
