Our Story

Who We Are

The Velantis Interstellar Commerce and Holding Group was founded in 2951 with one conviction: the 'verse is better when pilots look out for each other. What began as a small refueling operation in the Stanton system has grown into a full-spectrum service organization with reach across three star systems.

We are not a military outfit. We are not a mining conglomerate. We are a service organization — built on speed, professionalism, and the kind of operational discipline that keeps ships flying when everything else goes wrong.

Today, Velantis Group operates two entities: the main organization [VELANTIS] focused on commerce and logistics, and the private Velantis Luxury Concierge Club [VLCC] for members who expect more from the 'verse than it typically offers.

The Velantis Luxury Concierge Club is the private membership arm of Velantis Group — built for those who expect more than the 'verse typically offers.
